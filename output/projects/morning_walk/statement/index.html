Morning Walk · Statement

  • ← back to images

Since 2018 I've taken pictures as a part of my regular morning dog walks. Doing this every day alleviates the repetition of walking similar routes, and forces me to stay engaged with the subtle changes to light, weather, and the front yard landscapes that make up my small town.

This is an ongoing project, regularly updated via posts to Instagram.
